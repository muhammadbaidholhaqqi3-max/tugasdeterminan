Tugas Determinan & Invers Matriks

Soal 1

$$A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix}$$

Determinan:

$$\det(A) = (-7)(4) - (-5)(1) = -28 + 5 = -23$$

Invers:

$$A^{-1} = \frac{1}{-23} \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix}$$

Soal 2

$$A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$$

Ekspansi Baris 1:

$$\det(A) = 0\cdot M_{11} - 2\cdot M_{12} + (-3)\cdot M_{13}$$

$$M_{11} = \begin{vmatrix} -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} = -2$$

$$M_{12} = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} = 1$$

$$M_{13} = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} = 0$$

$$\det(A) = 0 - 2(1) + (-3)(0) = -2$$

Kesimpulan: det(A) ≠ 0 → memiliki invers

Soal 3

Determinan:

$$\det(A) = -128$$

Kesimpulan: memiliki invers
